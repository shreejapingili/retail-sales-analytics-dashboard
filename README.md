# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance using SQL, Excel, and Power BI. The objective was to identify key business trends, evaluate profitability, understand customer purchasing behavior, and provide actionable insights for decision-making.

The analysis was performed on the Superstore dataset containing approximately 9,700+ retail transactions across multiple product categories, regions, and customer segments.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data, but raw data alone does not provide actionable insights.

This project answers key business questions:

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which regions contribute the most sales and profit?
* Who are the top-performing customers?
* Which products drive business growth?
* How do sales and profits change over time?

---

## Key Insights

### Revenue Analysis

* Technology generated the highest sales revenue.
* Furniture contributed significant revenue but lower profitability.

### Regional Analysis

* The West region was the highest revenue contributor.
* Sales performance varied significantly across regions.

### Customer Analysis

* A small percentage of customers contributed a large share of total revenue.
* Identifying high-value customers can improve retention strategies.

### Profitability Analysis

* Discounts impacted profitability across several product categories.
* Certain products generated high sales volume but low profit margins.
